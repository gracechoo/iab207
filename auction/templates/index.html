About Us

The Record Factory is a leading, Brisbane based auction company. Founded by Romeet Puhar, Calum Hathaway, Grace Choo and Manpreet Singh, the site is made for Vinyl lovers to improve their collections. It provides a platform for users to recieve the best price for vinyl records that are no longer desired and also an opportunity for purchases of all types of records uploaded by anyone. We will always deliver a stable, fast and friendly user experience to our customers and best of all, are continually working hard to make sure the best range of music will always be available to our loyal customers.
